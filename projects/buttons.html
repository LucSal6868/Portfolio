Buttons

Created for the Game Music Jam 2024, which had the theme of 'Sisyphus', this game consists of pressing buttons of increasing difficulty and complexity.

I worked on the code and art, and after the base game was finished, my randomly assigned teammates worked on the music and sound effects.


While not incredibly complex in terms of actual design or code, I think the game makes up for it with some very fine tuned difficulty. The game starts disarmingly easy, but can quickly humble you if you let your guard down.


We ended up winning:


- Best Thematic Interpretation Award in Game Design

- Best Sound Design Award

- Best Game Award
