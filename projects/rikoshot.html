RIKOSHOT

A very small demo I made for fun, which is here because I think it showcases my ability to make scalable, manageable code. The game consists of combining different ammo types and magic types to make a gun with those properties.

You can combine 1 bullet type, which affects the damage, speed, reload time, and knockback of the gun. And you can add 2 magic types, 1 which will give the bullet an effect (make a puddle, freeze enemy, set them on fire, etc), and a second that will change the damage type.
I explain this system more in depth in the projects itch.io page that I linked at the bottom of the page.

Another thing I am very proud of is the inventory, which allows the player to stack items, split them, move them, etc. This is something that looks much easier than it is to implement.
