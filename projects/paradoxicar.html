PARADOXICAR

In this game, a copy of you spawns every 5 seconds that will mirror your movement from the moment the game started, up until the game ends. Interacting with a past version of yourself creates a time travel paradox, which ends the game.
Additionally, in order to keep the player on their toes and make sure they don't just move very slowly to cheese the game, a police car very slowly hunts the player down, and will end the game if touched.

Created for the Game Music Jam 2024, which had the theme of 'Paradox'. I ended up winning the 'Audience Favorite Award'.
