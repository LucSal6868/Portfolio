Banter Bots

This project was created for the KightHacks 2024 Hackathon, and won the "Best Use of Streamlit Award". It consisted of a website that used the Gemini AI & Steamlit API's to have 2 chatbots take different sides of an argument.
I worked on the backend and was in charge of the chatbots, while my partner worked on the front end, which used the Streamlit API. The website was hosted by Streamlit but no longer active due to inactivity.

The original goal of the project was to win the "Uselessness Award", thus the seemingly pointless topics the bots fight about, but our integration of Streamlit impressed the judges so we won that award instead.

The project is made entirely in Python, and links to it, and the Devpost can be found below.
